New Courses Introduced :

Sreenidhi Institute Of Science & Technology is a private university sponsored by the Sree Educational Group, approved by the All India Council For Tech
It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs like B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA in various disciplines. Apart from the main courses, The Science & Humanities Department comprises other domains like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English. This Department helps the engineering students in developing technical skills and communication skills. A research activity center is also set up for Ph.D. students to conduct research activities. It also got recognition by the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research as a reputed research organization. SNIST: Scholarships The SNIST committee created a portal Vidyalakshmi, for the students to access educational loans from banks. The Department of Finance, the Department of Education, and the Indian Banks Association helped the SNIST committee to develop the portal. M.Tech GATE qualified students with 90.8 percentile can avail scholarship during the duration of partaking in the course.
