Dear Recruiters,


It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology for the final placement process for the batch of 2021-22. The Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology (SNIST) endeavours to create better society by empowering young minds through value based education. At SNIST, we promote a culture of research. The young minds are encouraged to think differently and steered towards innovation. Our curriculum is based on a multi-disciplinary model to cater to the talent needs of the industry. Along with the strong faculty members, the Career Development Centre (CDC) supplements the effort by fostering personal, intellectual and experiential growth for students to ensure that they become well-rounded and responsible professionals of tomorrow.


It is a moment of pride for us to present you a group of vibrant, young, dynamic and talented batch of student managers ready to join the corporate world. Our student mix is selected through top 2-5% of EAMCET rankers and also through our assessment. A rigorous selection process ensuring that only the very best of the lot get into our SNIST. This selected batch is exposed to a curriculum that is thoroughly updated and in sync with the demand of modern business scenario. We encourage experience based learning.


SNIST is proud to have stood up to the expectations of several organizations over past 23 years and past recruiters have been regularly recruiting our students which stands as a testimony to the quality of our student talent. Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology is continuously rated one of the top engineering colleges in Telugu states. We assure you that our students are adequately equipped with personal and professional skills and Services, IT and product based knowledge to make them Industry ready. They are designed to excel in whichever sector they are put to work in. our ever growing alumni base spread over more than 10000+ organizations in many sectors around the country and abroad speak for this fact.
