Courses We offer

BTECH

" B.Tech or Bachelor of Technology is one of the prestigious and most popular courses opted by science students. It is a 4-year full-time undergraduate course and is offered in various disciplines such as Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, Electronics, and Information Technology. Skilled engineers are in high demand in India as well as in other countries. With India looking forward to expanding its technological advancements, the demand for engineers will further increase. A professional engineering undergraduate degree (B.Tech degree) is awarded to applicants after the completion of four years of academic study in the engineering field. A B.Tech degree is the starting point for a successful career in engineering.We offer undergraduate B.Tech programmes in CSE, CSE (AI&ML), AI&ML, AI&DS, IT, ECE, EEE, ME and CE;"

MTECH

"Master of Technology is a well-chosen course option for students who want to use their experience in science and technology. M Tech degrees help students move ahead in their careers and land up in good leading executive positions in industries, renowned companies and research organizations. Aspiring students need to qualify for various national, state or institute-level entrance exams so that they can get enrolled for the M Tech degree in a good college/university. M Tech course prepares the students to be skilled working professionals in diverse fields and to develop research oriented mindset with logical and analytical thinking capabilities. A Master of Technology degree holder has a huge number of opportunities from various sectors like IT, banking, education and business organizations etc."
